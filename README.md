I'm **Alexander (Sasha) Abramovich** тАФ an electronic music producer (**IDM ┬╖ minimal techno ┬╖ ambient ┬╖ experimental**), ~20 years in, 20+ releases as **Total Reboot**.

I also build my own **AI tools that *measure* music instead of hallucinating about it.** Not "AI says drop the bass at 2:30" тАФ but measurable, reproducible diagnostics where the creative call always stays with the author.

```
measured  тЖТ  what it means  тЖТ  up to you
```

That separation is the whole philosophy: the tool gives you the physics тАФ *"bass and mids are fighting over 200тАУ500 Hz, the low end buries the melody in the first half"* тАФ and never the decision.

> It started when another AI flat-out hallucinated about one of my tracks тАФ wrong duration, an arc that didn't exist, made-up gear. The real measurements proved it wrong. So I built a tool that **can't** lie: only what `librosa` and `Demucs` actually measure.
